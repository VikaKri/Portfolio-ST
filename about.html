Biography

Multi-lingual Pianist, Keyboardist, Arranger, and Composer based in London, UK.

Hi, I am Syune Tatevosyan. I've been studying music for more than 10 years (piano, music theory) as well as performing on stage.

I always tried to be connected with music at all stages of my life. I have organized many concerts and formed a music band in the early years of my studies, which developed my organizational skills. Since starting BIMM university I have been a part of several bands as a keyboard player and performed on the stages as O2 Islington, O2 Shepherd's Bush and The Water Rats. Moreover,during my final year I was working on a music album which was related to the impact of social media and technologies to our lives.

Above all, my biggest passion is composing and arranging music. I've been composing music on the piano since I was 9. I have a basic knowledge of sound engineering, but I'm keen to learn it at a more advanced level.

I am an educated Pianist and Producer with the kind of skill set that makes me a valuable asset to any team. I work with a wide range of artists in genres including Pop/Soul/R&B/Jazz/Classical/Electronic. I am very quick at writing Pop arrangements and the turnaround is fast.

Services I provide: Piano Arrangement, Piano Recording, Session Keys, Music Production, Songwriting, Midi Writing/Arrangement, String Arrangements, Orchestral Arrangements, Music for Film and TV.
I speak 4 languages (English, Russian, Latvian, Armenian) so don't hesitate to contact me in either of the four languages!
