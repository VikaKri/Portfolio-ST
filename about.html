Biography

Multi-lingual Pianist, Keyboardist, Arranger, and Composer based in London, UK.

Hi, I am Syune Tatevosyan. I've been studying music for more than 10 years(piano, music theory) as well as performing on stage.

I always tried to be connected with music at all stages of my life. I have organized many concerts and formed a music band in the early years of my studies, which developed my organizational skills. Since starting BIMM university I have been a part of several bands as a keyboard player and performed on thestagesasO2 Islington, O2 Shepherd's Bush and The Water Rats. Moreover,during my final year I was working on a musical bum which was related to the impact of social media and technologies to our lives.

Above all, my biggest passion is composing and arranging music.I've been composing music on the piano since I was9. I have a basic knowledge of sound engineering,but I'm keen to learn it at a more advanced level.
