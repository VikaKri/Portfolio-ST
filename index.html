About

Hi, I am Syune Tatevosyan. I've been studying music for more than 10 years (piano, music theory) as well as performing on stage.
I always tried to be connected with music at all stages of my life. I have organized many concerts and formed a music band in the early years of my studies. Since starting BIMM university I have been a part of several bands as a keyboard player and performed on the stages as O2 Islington, O2 Shepherd's Bush and The Water Rats.
